Mission

The mission of Seattle Academy's Outdoor Club is to get students active in the spaces around or community. We take hikes around many National Parks in the area, and seek to build our own community through engaging with the nature that surrounds us in Washington State.

Shoreline

Discovery Park and the Washingon State National Park are great places to explore. Their unique shoreline is a wow factor each time we go back.

Misty

The mysterious rainforests of Washington State offer a great outdoors experience. The serenity of the trees and quiet on the trails is unmatched by any area nearby.

Mountains

From the Olympic Range to the Cascade Range, all of the mountains in Washington Stand in peaceful waiting. Their open arms embrace ambitious hikers with a drive to push themselves.
